3-minute Demo Script — Life Insurance Recommendation Tool

Goal: Demonstrate the tool's value to agents and product managers — how it produces a defensible recommendation, captures a lead, and schedules consults.

Structure (3 minutes)

1) Intro (20s)
- One-line: "This is a quick, explainable life insurance recommendation tool built for advisors and Boston homeowners."
- Call out: DIME-based coverage, transparent decision rules, and Save-to-CRM + Calendly integration.

2) Scenario 1 — First-time homeowner, young family (60s)
- Inputs: Age 34, Income $90k, Mortgage $300k, Debt $20k, Children 2, Goal: Income Replacement
- Run: Show 'Get Recommendation' → Recommend: Level Term (20y) $750k
- Actions: Click ‘Download JSON’, ‘Copy Result’, then ‘Schedule Consultation’ (calendly.com/namaste1 opens with payload).
- Talking points: split-term strategy for mortgage + family, affordability, next steps (quote, placement).

3) Scenario 2 — Mid-career wealth accumulation (45s)
- Inputs: Age 42, Income $150k, No mortgage, Goal: Wealth Accumulation
- Run: Recommend: IUL (Indexed Universal Life) — show features: cash value growth, tax-free loans.
- Actions: Click ‘Save to CRM’ (demonstrates lead capture), then show one-pager (open onepager HTML) and mention agent follow-up script.
- Talking points: When to route to permanent products; underwriting considerations.

4) Scenario 3 — Legacy planning / older client (30s)
- Inputs: Age 60, Income $200k, Mortgage $0, Children 0, Goal: Wealth Transfer
- Run: Recommend: Whole Life (permanent) — show DIME calc minimal because debt/mortgage low.
- Actions: Print one-pager; point to underwriting snapshot and compliance note.
- Talking points: Use permanent solutions for estate goals; show how the tool reduces discovery work for advisors.

5) Close (10s)
- Recap: Fast, explainable, lead-ready — schedules, saves, and generates shareable one-pagers.
- Call to action: "We can map to your specific Transamerica/WFG products and wire CRM; shall I set up the pilot?"

Notes for presenter
- Emphasize explainability: show the DIME breakdown (unlock with an email) and the decision rules.
- Remind agents that product mappings are placeholders; confirm exact SKUs before quoting.
- Mention local authorship: Sanjeev Jha / Namaste Boston Homes for Boston SEO and trust.
